When I’m not studying, I like to stay active. I enjoy playing volleyball, swimming, and fishing. I also love watching movies and keeping up with tech trends—there’s always something new to check out. I’m fluent in Turkish, and my English is pretty good. As I move forward, I’m excited about how I can use what I’ve learned to contribute to the field of geomatics.
